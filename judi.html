Practice Areas

We specialize in the following areas

Corporate & Commercial Practice

Our offers deep expertise in all types of Corporate finance transactions owing to its involvement in a number of high-profile transactions involving local, international and multinational corporates.

Dispute Resolution Practice

We focus on providing legal advisory services to clients on the best, most effective and beneficial methods of resolving commercial disputes and claims using litigation, arbitration and other alternative dispute resolution methods of negotiation, mediation and conciliation at different stages as may be applicable.

Tax Practice Profile

Our tax lawyers are renowned for precise, commercially informed advice on all areas of Nigerian tax regime and the income and international tax regimes of OECD member states.

Shipping, Maritime & Dredging Legal Advisory Practice

We focus on providing legal advisory services relating to and concerning shipping and clearing, and forwarding agents, charterers, cargo interests including commodity traders and cargo insurers, hull and liability insurers, shipyards, necessaries men and port operators.
